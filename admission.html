SUPPORT

Admission FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our enrollment process.

01 What is the age criteria for Kindergarten?

Children should be 3 years and 6 months by the start of the academic year for KG1 admission.

02 Is there an entrance assessment?

Yes, we conduct age-appropriate assessments to understand each child's current learning level and social development.

03 Do you provide transportation?

Yes, the school offers safe and monitored transportation services covering most areas in and around Ibri.

04 When do admissions open?

Admissions typically open in February each year for the upcoming academic session starting in August.
